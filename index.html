About Us

Shehjar Multispeciality Hospital is located in the heart of Anantnag, Jammu & Kashmir, at Mominabad Ashajipora. We are committed to providing world-class healthcare services with advanced medical facilities and a highly experienced medical team.

Our hospital specializes in gynecology, obstetrics, pediatrics, internal medicine, and an array of surgical specialties to serve the diverse needs of our patients.
